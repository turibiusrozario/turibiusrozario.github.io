Dovetail Freebie (DT-FB) is a 5" free-style drone, though I expect to fly it
more as a cinematography drone. It has a FPV camera and an action camera (GoPro
Hero 11 Mini) for capturing footage. I took some pictures during the process of
building this. Here's in the early stage:

I attempted to make the build cleaner by placing the video transmitter
underneath the speed controller. This made the stack's gap small enough where,
upon connecting the 25 V battery, it shorted:

<div class="row">
    <div class="col-sm mt-3 mt-md-0">
        {% include figure.liquid loading="eager" path="assets/img/UAVs-DT-FB-after.jpg" title="Short circuit on the battery" class="img-fluid rounded z-depth-1" %}
    </div>
</div>
<div class="caption">
    I tested this with a multimeter to ensure there were no shorts, but plugging
    in a 6S LiPo battery was enough to go over the air gap for a short.
</div>
